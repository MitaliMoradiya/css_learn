FLIPKART INTRODUCE 'FLIPKART IDEAS'


Flipkart news | August 17,2019


Bengaluru, 16 August, 2019: Flipkart, India's leading e-commerce marketplace, has introduced 'Flipkart Ideas', a curated selection of content on its platform, to make consumers' shopping experience more meaningful by guiding them through their e-commerce purchase journey.


Flipkart Ideas will help bring the next 200 million consumers online, who will need easy discovery, inspiration and post purchase hand holding, as they navigate the benefits of e-commerce. The content from hundreds of brands and influencers will be available under 14 key interest areas including Fashion, health and fitness, gadgets, food and nutrition, home and living, etc. for consumers to choose from and customise their preferences Flipkart Ideas will engage consumers through videos, images, GIFs, stories, polls and quizzes.

Consumers continue to be at the centre of the Flipkart Ideas ecosystem with credible content being provided by subject-matter experts, brands and trusted publishers. This will help improve consumers' shopping experiences with credible, inspiring and useful content, which will not only educate but also inspire them in their purchase journey.
